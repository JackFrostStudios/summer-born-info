Review palette

Direction C6

Use electric violet to make the homepage feel bold, current, and clearly public.

This option keeps Direction C's campaign-style hierarchy intact, but pushes the colour system toward a more contemporary event-poster energy with peach and cream counterbalance.

Why it stands apart

The page feels highly visible and modern without losing the structure of a service homepage.

Distinct voice

Violet gives the masthead a clearly different emotional register from the earlier civic, rust, or forest palettes.

Balanced contrast

Peach softens the accent system enough to keep the page energetic rather than abrasive.

Campaign framing

The stacked site header, banner cues, and bold information blocks keep the option rooted in the Direction C family.

Implementation note

This is the most contemporary and personality-led palette of the new C-family colour round.
