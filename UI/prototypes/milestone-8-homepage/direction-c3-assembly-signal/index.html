Direction C3

Make the homepage feel like a rally point with clearer framing and stronger contrast blocks.

What changes here

Sharper signal blocks

Each content area lands as a distinct public message, helping the page feel active without losing structure.

Community emphasis

The colour system and language suggest collective action and local momentum more than institutional calm.
